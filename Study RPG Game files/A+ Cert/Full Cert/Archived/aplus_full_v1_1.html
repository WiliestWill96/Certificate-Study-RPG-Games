📖 PLAYER GUIDE
⚔️ HOW TO PLAY
You are a Sysadmin battling across both CompTIA A+ exams. All 12 worlds are open from the start — Core 1 and Core 2. Answer questions correctly to deal damage. Answer wrong and take a hit. Clear all 5 stages of a world to fight the World Boss, clearing the world. Defeating both sets of 6 worlds unlocks the Core 1 Kernel and Core 2 Kernel respectively. Defeat both Kernels and complete both Field Ops zones to unlock The Proving Ground.
🗺️ CORE 1 WORLDS (220-1101)
📱 MobileMobile devices, display tech, connectivity, MDM, troubleshooting.
🌐 C1 NetworkingOSI model, protocols, IP addressing, wireless, ports.
⚙️ HardwareComponents, form factors, RAM, storage, CPUs, peripherals.
☁️ CloudCloud models, virtualization, Type 1/2 hypervisors.
🔧 Troubleshooting6-step methodology, hardware and network troubleshooting.
📕 Core 1 Terms220-1101 acronyms and key terminology.
💀 Core 1 KernelUnlocks after clearing all 6 Core 1 worlds. Defeat to unlock C1 Field Ops.
📟 C1 Field OpsPost-boss endgame. Mixed Core 1 content. Unlocks after Core 1 Kernel.
🗺️ CORE 2 WORLDS (220-1202)
🖥️ Operating SystemsWindows, macOS, Linux — installation, config, CLI tools, file systems.
🔒 SecurityMalware, attacks, encryption, hardening, authentication.
🐛 Software TroubleshootingWindows errors, malware removal, mobile issues, browser problems.
📋 Operational ProceduresBackup, documentation, safety, change management, compliance.
📡 C2 NetworkingNetworking from an OS/security perspective — DNS, DHCP, VPN, ports.
📗 Core 2 Terms220-1202 acronyms and key terminology.
💀 Core 2 KernelUnlocks after clearing all 6 Core 2 worlds. Defeat to unlock C2 Field Ops.
📟 C2 Field OpsPost-boss endgame. Mixed Core 2 content. Unlocks after Core 2 Kernel.
🏆 THE PROVING GROUND
The ultimate endgame. Unlocks after all 12 worlds cleared, both Kernels defeated, and both Field Ops zones completed. 8 stages of combined A+ knowledge.
S1: 💻 HW + OSHardware meets Operating Systems.
S2: 🌐 Net + Net SecCore 1 Networking meets Core 2 Network Security.
S3: 📱 Mobile + OpsMobile devices meet Operational Procedures.
S4: ☁️ Cloud + SecCloud concepts meet Security.
S5: 🔧 Trbl + SW TrblCore 1 Troubleshooting meets Core 2 Software Troubleshooting.
S6: 📚 TerminologyBoth acronym zones combined — Core 1 and Core 2 terms.
S7: ⭐ The GeneralsElite enemies drawing from all 12 zones.
S8: 👑 Final ExamFace THE CERTIFICATION AUTHORITY — boss HP:1200, ATK:30.
📊 COMBAT
✅ CorrectDeal ATK + streak bonus damage. Streak cap grows with level.
❌ WrongEnemy deals ATK damage. Streak resets to 0.
💥 CRIT15% base chance for +4 bonus damage. Boosted by Lucky Charm and Overclock.
⏱️ TimerRunning out counts as wrong. Answering in under 1 second = speed bonus.
📈 PROGRESSION
⭐ XPfloor(level × 1.8) + stage bonus per correct answer.
🆙 Level Up+5 max HP, ATK = floor(level × 2.5) + 5.
💰 Goldfloor(4 + level × 0.6) + 0–2 per correct answer.
👑 Level 30Auto-awards Legend's Cache (+3 ATK, +10 HP, +15% XP) + ⚡ LEGEND title.
🏪 SHOP & BAG
Shop opens between every battle. Items unlock by level. Bag starts at 15 slots, +1 per world cleared (12 possible), +3 per Bag Expander. Permanent items are bought once and kept forever.

👾 SECRET BOSS
Appears after any stage if you missed questions. HP scales with missed count (cap 300). ATK capped at 70% of your ATK so it's never instantly lethal. Defeat for bonus gold + XP. Resume continues to next stage.
⚔️ SURVIVAL ZONE
Unlocks after clearing all 6 worlds in either Core 1 or Core 2. No items allowed.
❤️ EnduranceAnswer until HP runs out.
🔥 Perfect RunOne wrong answer ends the run.

Access from the main menu. Use the ALL / CORE 1 / CORE 2 buttons to filter by exam. Then choose a specific domain tab or Missed-Only mode. Cards flip to show the full explanation.
