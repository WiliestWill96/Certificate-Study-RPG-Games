📖 PLAYER GUIDE
⚔️ HOW TO PLAY
All 6 main worlds are open from the start. Each world has 5 stages plus a World Boss. Stages unlock in order — clear S1 to unlock S2, etc. Stage 5 is The Generals (4 elite enemies). Clear all 6 worlds to unlock The Kernel.
🗺️ WORLDS & STAGES
📊 BattlesS1=12, S2=10, S3=8, S4=6, S5=4 Generals → World Boss
⚠️ Enemy scalingEnemies scale with your player level regardless of which world you choose.
🔄 NG+Cleared worlds can be replayed. Enemies scale up each run (capped at 3 runs).
📊 COMBAT
✅ CorrectDeal ATK + streak bonus damage. Streak cap grows with your level.
❌ WrongEnemy deals ATK damage to you. Streak resets to 0.
💥 CRIT15% base chance for +4 bonus damage. Boosted by Lucky Charm and Overclock.
⏱️ TimerRunning out counts as wrong. Under 1 second = speed bonus.
📈 PROGRESSION
⭐ XPfloor(level × 1.8) + stage index bonus per correct answer.
🆙 Level Up+5 max HP, ATK = floor(level × 2.5) + 5, streak cap grows.
💰 Goldfloor(4 + level × 0.6) + 0–2 per correct answer.
👑 Level 30Auto-awards Legend's Cache (+3 ATK, +10 HP, +15% XP), +500g, and ⚡ LEGEND title.
🏪 SHOP & ITEMS
Shop appears between every battle. Items unlock by player level. Permanent items are bought once and kept forever. Consumables go in your bag (starts 15 slots, +1 per world cleared, +3 per Bag Expander owned).

👾 BOSSES & SECRETS
💀 World BossUnlocks after all 5 stages cleared. Completing it clears the world.
😈 Secret BossAppears after each stage if you missed questions. HP scales with missed count (up to 300). ATK is capped at 70% of your ATK so it's never instantly lethal. Defeat for bonus gold + XP. Resume continues to next stage.
💀 THE KERNELFinal Boss. Unlocks after all 6 worlds cleared. HP scales with your level (200 + level×20, so L30 = 800 HP). Defeating it unlocks Field Ops Command.
🎖️ FIELD OPS COMMAND
Post-game content. Unlocks after defeating The Kernel. Five stages of increasing difficulty drawing questions from all six domains. Clears the way for the ultimate challenge.
🎖️ Tiers 1–512→10→8→6→4 battles. All questions drawn from all 6 main domains.
💀 Sysadmin SupremeField Ops World Boss. Defeating it grants the Field Ops Master achievement.
⚔️ SURVIVAL ZONE
Unlocks after clearing all 6 worlds. No items allowed.

🔥 Perfect RunOne wrong answer ends the run.
⏱️ Time Trial90 seconds. Correct answers add 2s. Score as many as possible.
🏆 RewardsGold = correct × 3, XP = correct × 12. All-time best streak tracked.
