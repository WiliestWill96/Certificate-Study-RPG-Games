📖 PLAYER GUIDE
⚔️ HOW TO PLAY
Each world has 5 stages plus a World Boss. Stages unlock in order within each world. Stage 5 is The Generals — 12 increasingly tough enemies. Defeat the World Boss after all 5 stages to fully clear the world. The Kernel unlocks once all 6 main worlds are cleared.
📊 COMBAT BASICS
✅ CorrectYou deal ATK damage. Streak bonuses add extra damage.
❌ WrongEnemy deals ATK damage to you. Streak resets to 0.
💥 CRIT15% chance to deal bonus damage on correct answers.
⏱️ TimerEach question has a timer. Running out counts as wrong.
⚡ SpeedAnswer in under 1 second for a speed bonus!
📈 PROGRESSION
⭐ XPGain XP per correct answer. XP scales up as you clear more zones.
🆙 Level UpEach level raises your MAX HP and ATK stat.
💰 GoldEarn 5–7 gold per correct answer. Use gold at the shop.
🔓 ZonesWorlds unlock in order: Mobile → Net → HW → Cloud → Trbl → Acronym.
🏪 THE SHOP
A shop appears between every battle. Items unlock as you clear stages — deeper stages unlock better gear. Permanent items (PERM) are bought once and last forever. Consumables go in your bag and are used during battles. Your bag starts at 15 slots and grows +1 for every new zone you clear.
🎒 BAG & ITEMS
🧪 Minor Potion+15 HP. Always available.
💊 Super Potion+30 HP. Unlocks after Zone 2.
🌿 Max Potion+50 HP. Unlocks after Zone 3.
✨ ElixirFull HP restore. Unlocks after Zone 4.
💎 Revive StoneAuto-revives you at 10 HP when you'd die. Passive.
📜 Hint ScrollRemoves 1 wrong answer from choices.
🔮 Oracle OrbRemoves 2 wrong answers from choices.
📖 Study TomeReveals the correct answer.
🛡️ Iron ShieldBlocks 50% of the next hit you take.
🪨 Bulwark ShardNext 3 hits deal 50% less damage.
⏳ Time Shard+8 seconds on the timer. Always available.
⌛ Time Crystal+15 seconds on the timer.
⏩ Skip ScrollSkips the current question. Counts as a wrong answer.
🏅 PERMANENT UPGRADES
Permanent items are bought once and never lost. They upgrade your character forever.
⚔️ Sharp Blade+3 ATK permanently.
🔩 Iron WillAll damage taken -2 permanently.
🕐 Chronowatch+4 seconds added to every question timer.
🍀 Lucky Charm+10% crit chance permanently.
📚 Study Guide+30% XP per correct answer permanently.
🧠 Neural Band-1 damage on every hit (stacks with Iron Will). Unlocks: Clear Mobile Zone.
📡 Signal Amp+5% bonus gold per correct answer. Unlocks: Clear Net Zone.
💾 Quantum CacheRestore +10 HP at the start of each zone. Unlocks: Clear Cloud Zone.
🔍 Lexicon Lens+5 seconds on every question timer. Unlocks: Clear Acronym Zone.
⚡ Overclock+2 ATK and +5% crit chance. Unlocks: Level 15.
👾 BOSSES & SECRETS
👑 Zone BossEvery zone has a boss at battle 10. Higher HP, tougher ATK, harder questions.
😈 Secret BossAppears after clearing a zone if you missed any questions. Boss HP = questions correct, ATK = questions missed. Defeat it for bonus gold and XP.
💀 THE KERNELThe Final Boss. Unlocks after clearing all 6 main zones. Defeating it unlocks the Field Ops Command zone.
⚔️ SURVIVAL ZONE
Unlocks after clearing all 6 main zones. Three modes — no items allowed:
❤️ EnduranceAnswer until your HP runs out. Wrong answers deal damage. Damage increases the further you go.
🔥 Perfect RunOne wrong answer ends your run. Perfection only.
⏱️ Time Trial90 seconds. Answer as many questions correctly as possible. Each correct answer adds 2 seconds.
🏆 RewardsEarn 1.5g and 5 XP per correct answer in survival.
🔄 NG+ & REPLAYABILITY
Cleared zones can be replayed for more gold and XP. Each replay increases enemy HP and ATK and shows the run number and difficulty multiplier on the map. Questions you answered correctly are removed from the pool — you'll see fresh questions each run.
💾 SAVING & CROSS-SAVE
The game auto-saves after every battle. Use the 💾 SAVE button on the map to save manually. To transfer your save to another device, use 🔄 CROSS-SAVE from the main menu or map to export or import a save code.
📚 STUDY DECK
Access the Study Deck from the main menu at any time. Filter by domain or missed-only to review your weak areas. Cards flip to show the explanation. Great for studying before attempting a harder zone.
